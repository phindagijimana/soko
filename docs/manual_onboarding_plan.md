# Manual Onboarding Plan

## Goal
Get the first small pilot group onto the platform without product complexity.

## Suggested pilot cohort
- 10 farmers
- 5 buyers
- 1 coordinator who helps with signup, OTP, and listing creation

## Manual onboarding steps
1. Collect each participant's name, phone number, role, and location.
2. Register each person through the app or with the backend `/users` endpoint.
3. Request OTP for each phone number.
4. Help the participant sign in.
5. For farmers, create their first listing together.
6. For buyers, walk through browsing and placing one order.
7. Record any confusion, delays, or missed notifications.

## What to capture during onboarding
- Could they understand the difference between farmer and buyer roles?
- Did phone authentication feel easy enough?
- Did they know what to type for crop, quantity, and price?
- Did they trust the listing and order flow?

## Do not do yet
- Do not add logistics.
- Do not add payments.
- Do not redesign the workflow during the first pilot.
